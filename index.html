What Is real estate

  • Real estate industry is buying and then selling land along with buildings and houses. Real estate is a form of real property. Real estate business or real estate agents sell property to buyers. Real estate also refers to producing, buying and selling real estate.
  • Investing is involved in real estate. Real estate investing involves the purchase, ownership, management, rental and/or sale of real estate for profit.
  • Investors can make money in real estate by becoming a landlord of a rental property. People who are flippers, buy up undervalued real estate, then fixing it up, and selling it for more.
