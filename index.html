Overview

The Real estate industry is buying and then selling land along with buildings and houses. A Real estate is a form of real property. Real estate businesses or real estate agents sell the property to buyers. Real estate also refers to producing, buying, and selling real estate.

Investing is involved in real estate. Real estate investing involves the purchase, ownership, management, rental, and/or sale of real estate for profit.

Investors can make money in real estate by becoming a landlord of a rental property. People who are flippers, buy up undervalued real estate, then fix it up, and sell it for more.

    Matterport: Camera & App

  • Matterport is a three-dimensional camera system you can use to create realistic, fully immersive experiences. They are where people can look in a picture and feel like they're in the house using a three-dimensional camera.
  • Matterport had raised just under $63 million before this and had been valued at around $207 million, according to PitchBook estimates.
  • Matterport was founded in 2011.
  • Matterport Capture app allows you to make a 3D model Dollhouse View Tour of any space, from a room in a home or hotel to an entire office building or factory using a 3D camera (Pro2 3D Camera), a 360° spherical camera, a lidar camera, or your iPhone or iPad!
